escape the screen

Interactive spaces and projections

Digital Prisoners is a collaboration between dance artist & film-maker Naomi Morris and artist & technologist Neil C Smith.

We make really social media — digital projects that escape from the screen; animating and encouraging movement & interplay within real-world spaces.

Meeting Point returned to Bonn Square, Oxford in March as part of Dancin' Oxford 2014. Meeting Point is an interactive projection that allows two strangers to dance "together" from opposite sides of a public square. Premiering in 2014 we have 8 new scenes, and a variety of guest music from Tiger Mendoza, Flights of Helios, Ben Mowat and Lee Christian.

Meeting Point is currently available for touring - please get in touch for more information.
