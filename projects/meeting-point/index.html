Meeting Point

March, 2014

Step into a live, interactive dance film.

A cinematic transformation of a public square, Meeting Point allows two strangers to move together remotely from opposite ends of the space. Their combined movement, captured, merged and creatively manipulated, projected onto a single point in the urban landscape.

In 2014 Dancin' Oxford commissioned us to develop an updated version of Meeting Point (2013) for Bonn Square in central Oxford. We developed eight new scenes, and incorporated music from a variety of Oxford musicians including Tiger Mendoza, Flights of Helios, Ben Mowat and Lee Christian.

The work uses uses two infra-red CCTV cameras, custom computer software and a large-scale projector.  It allows the two participants to interact with each other and, through motion detection, with the other audio and visual elements of each section.

Meeting Point is currently available for touring - if you'd be interested in hosting it, please get in touch.
